Welcome to the BETA Pack Simulator!
Currently a work in development allowing users to virtually open packs!


How it Works:

1: Select a Set
2: A Set of Cards will be selected from that set to create a pack
3: Regardless of opening the pack or not by click the next pack will generate a new pack. These packs will closely resemble to an actual pack in terms of ratios and probability.
4: By pressing reset case, it will reset the entire case of cards that you have opened.
5: By clicking a card, you reveal the card as well as show the information of that card
Note: Expected Case Ratios have not been implemented. This is still a work in progress.
